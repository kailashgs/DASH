Our Story

Born from a simple belief.

GOOD GRUB started in a small bakery in Bengaluru, India, where our founder — a dad tired of watching his kids pick the crust off their sandwiches and throw it away — decided enough was enough.

Why make bread with the part nobody wants? So he didn't. After months of recipe testing, failing, tweaking, and more taste tests than any family should endure, the SoftWhite Crustless Bread was born — baked entirely free of edges, entirely full of the soft center everyone actually loves.

Every loaf is still baked in Bengaluru using the same original recipe: simple, quality ingredients, no shortcuts, no artificial nonsense. Just bread the way it should be — soft, simple, and made for GOOD GRUB.
